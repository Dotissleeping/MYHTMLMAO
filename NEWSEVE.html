RECENT NEWS 2024


Students in public elementary and secondary schools are scheduled to go on a five-day mid-year break starting Jan. 24.

Based on DepEd Order no. 32 series of 2023 or the Implementing Guidelines on the School Calendar and Activities for the School Year 2023-2024, the mid-year break for the current school year is scheduled on Jan. 24 to 26 (Wednesday to Friday) and on Jan. 29 to 30 (Monday and Tuesday).
